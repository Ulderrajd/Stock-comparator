Residual Income (Asset-Based) Method. Formula: IV = BVPS + Residual Income / (ke − g), where Residual Income = EPS − ke × BVPS. This is the single-stage Residual Income Model — it values the stock at book value plus the present value of economic profit (earnings above the cost of equity). If residual income is negative, the stock trades at a discount to book value. Requires BVPS and EPS.
